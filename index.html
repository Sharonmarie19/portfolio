About

It was never a dream of mine to become a designer. At least not visually, anyway. Yes. I wanted to design, but I wanted to use my words, a pen and paper. I wanted to design a world that people could imagine and get lost in. A world that would make people be feel better and be better. But fate is funny. Today, I am a designer. I only design in a different way but for the same reason. Now I design visuals and experiences that can help people be better and do better. Now, I design with colors and shapes.

Case Studies

Budget Break is a budgeting app that allows its users to create a budget, save money, and see their spending habits. My design decisions were based on what users were looking for in a budgeting app. Simple and easy to use. I decided to use the concept of Flat Design.

WeGame takes trivia to a whole new level. Not only can you play this game alone, but you can recruit your own family and friends to play the games you created. Let the competition begin!
